Golden Sands Activities

All activities are included with your resort stay.

Spa

Relax with a private massage, hydrotherapy, and beauty treatments. Contact us to book your appointment.

Exercise Classes

Join us for yoga, spinning, and strength training classes daily. Ask the concierge for the current week's schedule.

Outdoor Activities

Try paddleboarding, snorkeling, and volleyball. Gear can be rented from the lifeguard stand on the north side of the beach.
